# Dine Privacy Policy

**Effective: 2026-04-19**

This Privacy Policy describes how Dine ("we," "us") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you use the Dine mobile application ("the App"). If you have questions, email [sankalans@gmail.com](mailto:sankalans@gmail.com).

## Information we collect

### Account information
- Email address, display name, and profile photo (via Sign in with Apple or account setup)
- Optional phone number (for contact-based friend matching)
- Optional Venmo username (used only to construct payment request deep links)

### Content you create
- Photos you take of food or receipts
- Captions, dish names, ratings, and restaurant names on posts
- Comments, likes, and tags on other users' content

### Device and technical information
- Device type, iOS version, app version
- Product analytics events (which screens you view, which actions you take)
- Push notification token (if you grant notification permission)

### Information from your device (with your permission)
- **Camera**: to take food photos and scan receipts
- **Photos**: to upload food photos you select
- **Contacts**: to help you find friends already using Dine (phone numbers only)
- **Notifications**: to send push notifications about likes, comments, and tags

You can grant, deny, or revoke each of these permissions at any time in iOS **Settings → Dine**.

## How we use information

We use your information to:
- Provide the App and its features (posting, social feed, bill splitting)
- Scan and parse receipts. Images are transmitted to Google Cloud Vision (OCR) and OpenAI (structured extraction) by our server. We do not store receipt images on our servers beyond the processing request.
- Match phone numbers from your Contacts to existing Dine users. We store normalized phone numbers you have imported; we do not upload your full Contacts list.
- Send push notifications relevant to your account
- Understand how the App is used and improve it (aggregate analytics)

## Third-party service providers

We use the following service providers, who process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies:

- **Supabase** — database, authentication, and file storage hosting
- **Google Cloud Vision** — receipt OCR
- **OpenAI** — receipt parsing and taste-profile embeddings
- **Mixpanel** — product analytics
- **Expo** — push notification delivery and over-the-air app updates
- **Apple** — Sign in with Apple (authentication only)

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** share your information with advertisers.

## Bill splitting and Venmo

When you send a Venmo payment request from Dine, the App opens Venmo using a deep link that includes the amount and memo. We do not transmit payment data to Venmo; Venmo receives this data directly from your device via the Venmo app.

## Your choices and rights

- **Permissions**: revoke Camera, Photos, Contacts, or Notification access any time in iOS Settings → Dine.
- **Account deletion**: delete your account from within the App (Settings → Delete Account). This removes your posts, ratings, profile, and personal data associated with your account.
- **Data access request**: email [sankalans@gmail.com](mailto:sankalans@gmail.com) to request a copy of your data or ask for it to be deleted.

## Data retention

We retain account data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain certain records.

## Children

Dine is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, contact us and we will delete it.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Effective" date above and, for material changes, notify you in the App.

## Contact

Questions, concerns, or requests: [sankalans@gmail.com](mailto:sankalans@gmail.com).
